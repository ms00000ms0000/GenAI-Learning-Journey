# Day 3 - Embeddings

## What are Embeddings?
Embeddings are numerical vector representations of text that capture semantic meaning and relationships between words or sentences.

## Vectors
A vector is a list of numbers used to represent text mathematically so machines can process and compare meaning.

## Semantic Search
Semantic search finds information based on meaning and context rather than exact keyword matches.

## Similarity Search
Similarity search identifies documents or text chunks that are most semantically similar to a user's query.

## Cosine Similarity
Cosine Similarity measures the angle between vectors to determine how closely related two pieces of text are.

## Importance in RAG
Embeddings allow RAG systems to retrieve relevant information using meaning-based search instead of keyword matching.

## Key Learning
Embeddings transform text into vectors, enabling semantic retrieval and forming the foundation of modern RAG systems.
